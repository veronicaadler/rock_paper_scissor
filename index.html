Rock Paper Scissors is a hand game usually played by two people similar to flipping a coin, drawing straws or throwing dice. Your objective is to defeat your opponent by choosing 1 of 3 hand signals:

Rock wins against scissors.
Scissors win against paper.
Paper wins against rock.

If you and your opponent choose the same signal, the game will result in a tie.
